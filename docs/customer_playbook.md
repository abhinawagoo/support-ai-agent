# Customer playbook (demo docs)

## Tiers

- **Enterprise**: dedicated CSM, annual renewal, Drive-stored contracts under file ids like `demo-contract-q2`.
- **Starter**: self-serve billing in app; refunds within 14 days per policy below.

## Billing & refunds

- Plans renew on the subscription anniversary.
- Refund window: **14 days** from first charge for Starter; Enterprise negotiated in contract (see Drive file).

## CRM hygiene

- Always link **contact email** before quoting discounts.
- Escalate if `health_score` drops below 40.
